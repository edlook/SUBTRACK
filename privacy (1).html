Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Introduction

SubTrack Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Our Commitment: We believe your data belongs to you. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information We Collect

SubTrack Pro collects and stores the following information:

  • Subscription Data: Information about subscriptions you manually add, including service names, prices, billing cycles, and renewal dates.
  • Account Information: If you create an account for cloud sync, we collect your email address and encrypted password.
  • Preferences: Your chosen settings, theme preferences, and notification configurations.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Display and manage your subscription tracking dashboard
  • Send payment reminder notifications (locally, through your browser)
  • Sync your data across devices (if you opt into cloud sync)
  • Calculate spending analytics and insights
  • Improve the extension's functionality

Data Storage

Local Storage: By default, all your subscription data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device unless you enable cloud sync.

Cloud Sync (Optional): If you create an account, your data is securely stored in Firebase (Google Cloud) with industry-standard encryption. Your data is associated only with your account and is not accessible to other users.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Firebase/Google Cloud: If you use cloud sync, your encrypted data is stored on Firebase infrastructure.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

Permissions Explained

Our extension requests the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your subscriptions and settings locally.
  • Alarms: To schedule payment reminder notifications.
  • Notifications: To display payment reminders.
  • Host Permissions: To optionally detect subscription services you visit (this feature is optional and can be disabled).

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored by the extension
  • Export: Download your data as CSV at any time
  • Delete: Remove your data by uninstalling the extension or deleting your account
  • Opt-out: Use the extension without creating an account (local-only mode)

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication through Firebase
  • No storage of payment card information

Children's Privacy

SubTrack Pro is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
