Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Introduction

SubTrack Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Our Commitment: Your data stays on your device. We do not collect, sell, rent, or share your personal information with anyone. Period.

Information We Store

SubTrack Pro stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Subscription Data: Information about subscriptions you manually add, including service names, prices, billing cycles, and renewal dates.
  • Preferences: Your chosen settings, theme preferences, region, and notification configurations.

Important: This data is stored using Chrome's local storage API and never leaves your device. We have no access to it.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be crystal clear about what we don't do:

  • We do NOT collect your email address or any account information
  • We do NOT collect your passwords or login credentials
  • We do NOT access your payment or credit card information
  • We do NOT track your browsing history
  • We do NOT use any analytics or tracking services
  • We do NOT show ads
  • We do NOT send your data to any servers

How Your Data Is Used

Your locally stored information is used exclusively to:

  • Display and manage your subscription tracking dashboard
  • Send payment reminder notifications (locally, through your browser)
  • Calculate spending analytics and insights
  • Compare regional pricing for services
  • Apply your chosen theme and preferences

Data Storage

All your subscription data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We cannot see or access your data
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all data
  • You can export your data as CSV anytime for backup

Permissions Explained

Our extension requests the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your subscriptions and settings locally on your device.
  • Alarms: To schedule payment reminder notifications.
  • Notifications: To display payment reminders before billing dates.
  • Host Permissions: To optionally detect subscription services you visit and auto-fill pricing. This feature can be disabled in settings.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all data stored by the extension anytime
  • Export: Download your data as CSV from the Analytics section
  • Delete: Remove all data by uninstalling the extension
  • Control: Enable or disable auto-detection in Privacy settings

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security
  • No data transmission means no interception risk
  • We never store any payment card information

Children's Privacy

SubTrack Pro is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
